The "Zero-Waste" Supermarket: Dynamic Perishable Management
Focus: Circular Economy & Retail.

The Problem: Supermarkets throw away tons of food because they can't sell it before the expiry date. Discounting is often manual and too late.

The System:

Data Science Layer (The Analyst):

Ingest: Inventory logs, expiration dates, historical sales velocity, and competitor pricing.

Analyze: Identify "At-Risk" items (e.g., 500 yogurts expiring in 2 days).

Machine Learning Layer (The Optimizer):

Model: Reinforcement Learning (DQN) or Bayesian Demand Forecasting.

Task: Calculate the optimal discount curve. (e.g., "If we drop price by 10% now, we sell 50 units. If we wait, we sell 0.")

GenAI Layer (The Marketer):

Model: GPT-4o / Gemini Pro.

Action: Automatically generate targeted app notifications or digital shelf labels: "Flash Save! These strawberries are perfectly ripe for a smoothie. 50% off for the next 2 hours only. Here is a 5-minute recipe."
